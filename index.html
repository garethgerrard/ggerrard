About

Gareth Gerrard is a film director. His films explore self and shadow, memory, and the quieter shades of the human condition.

His background in photography, design, video production, and music informs a visual language that is precise, considered, and shaped as much by absence as by image.

He is currently developing a new short film while also working on feature-length projects.
